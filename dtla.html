Downtown Los Angeles

My body may be from Orange County but my soul's from DTLA.

Posted by Emily Sim on March 21, 2016

Itinerary:

  1. 1000 Eggslut in Grand Central Market
  2. 1030 McConnell's Icecream
  3. 1140 Angel's Flight
  4. 1220 Pershing Square to Metro Center/7th Street
  5. 1300 Heather at Philz on Hope/8th
  6. 1600 Lawyer Meeting in Wilshire/Normandie
  7. 1800 Stereoscope Coffee Co. with Mama

La Casa Verde used to be here...

Every time I go into Grand Central Market, a new stand has opened its doors to a crowd of angsty teens and raging hipsters. I'm guilty of being a part of this, too. After all, I'm a part of the frequently joked about "millenial" generation. I can't help but think about the people that establishments like "Eggslut" are displacing. (oh so clearly marketed at the Latino community that was previously inhabiting DTLA near Pershing Square). Gentrification is a real issue in DTLA and so many in their early 20's are unfazed because we're too busy taking care of our instagram aesthetic..

Mediocre sandwiches with a side of gentrification.

Mama Sim

Despite the legal trouble we've been going through, it's always nice to be in the nest you started out from. Love her, love the aesthetic, love Stereoscope.

The barista came over and asked if our Americanos were ok because my mom and I have perpetual RBF's.
